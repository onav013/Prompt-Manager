# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Avoid harassment, insults, and discriminatory behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
